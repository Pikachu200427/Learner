Navigating to a Question :

  1. Click on the question number to navigate to that particular question.
  2. To go back to the previous question, click on the 'Back' button.
  3. To go to the next question, click on the 'Next' button.
  4. To mark a question for review, click on the respective button next to the question number.
  5. To unmark a question for review, click on the 'Remove Review' button next to the question number.
  6. To submit your answers, click on the 'Submit' button.

Answering to a Question :

  1. Choose the correct option from the provided options for each question.
  2. To select multiple options, hold down the Ctrl or Command key while clicking on the options.
  3. To clear all selected options, click on the 'Clear' button.
  4. To add a comment to a question, type in the comment box and click on the 'Add Comment' button.
  5. To remove a comment from a question, click on the 'Remove Comment' button next to the comment.
  6. To edit a comment, click on the 'Edit' button next to the comment and make your changes.
  7. To submit your answers, click on the 'Submit' button.
